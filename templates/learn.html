Practice Instructions
{% if pose_choice == 'warrior' %}

To practice Warrior Pose effectively, ground your feet firmly, align your front knee over your ankle, and keep your back leg straight. Engage your core, relax your shoulders, and focus on deep, steady breathing.

{% elif pose_choice == 'raised' %}

For Raised Hands Pose, begin with your feet hip-width apart. Slowly raise your arms overhead, elongate your spine, and engage your core. Maintain steady breathing and relaxed shoulders throughout the pose.

{% endif %}
